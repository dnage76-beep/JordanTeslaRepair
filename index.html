Tesla Repair You Can Trust

Jordan Horwitz is an engineer by trade and a Tesla enthusiast by passion. After years of working on Teslas and rebuilding them from the ground up, he founded Jordan's Tesla Repair to give Tesla owners an alternative to the dealership -- one that delivers the same quality at a better price.

Every repair uses genuine OEM parts sourced directly from Tesla. From corrosion protection and chassis work to full rebuilds, Jordan treats every vehicle like it's his own. His engineering background means nothing gets overlooked -- every bolt torqued to spec, every panel aligned to factory standards.

Jordan is also passionate about the Tesla community, mentoring friends and fellow enthusiasts who want to learn how to maintain and repair their own vehicles.
